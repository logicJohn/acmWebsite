F.A.Q

  • What is ECU Spark?

    ECU Spark is a day of fun and learning. You'll become a better programmer, meet awesome people, and make cool stuff. There will also be opportunities to win cool prizes and network. Collaborate, Create, Innovate

  • Do I have to attend the workshops or STEM Gala?

    No, you are welcome to only attend the final ECU Spark event on the 9th but the workdays and Gala leading up to the main event give you a headstart to meet new people, find a group, and come up with great ideas together before the event day. It is a particularly great option if you are new to this type of event and would like more guidance or if you would like to free up more time during the main event to network and attend talks rather than working on your project.

  • Will there be food?

    Food and snacks will be provided throughout the event thanks to the Honors College. There are many delicious places to eat nearby campus as well

  • Will there be prizes?

    There will be several prizes offered for those of you who present projects!

  • Is there a minimum age?

    You must be 18 to attend

  • What should I bring?

    Bring anything you need to code or create a project including your computer and power cord

  • Do I have to be a Business or Computer Science major?

    Nope, all majors are welcome!
