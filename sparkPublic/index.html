F.A.Q

  • What is ECU Sparks?

    ECU Sparks is a day of fun and learning. You'll become a better programmer, meet awesome people, and make cool stuff. There will also be opportunities to win cool prizes and network.

  • Will there be food?

    Food and snacks will be provided throughout the event thanks to the Honors College. There are many delicious places to eat nearby campus as well.

  • Will there be prizes?

    There will be several prizes offered for those of you who present projects!

  • Is there a minimum age?

    You must be 18 to attend

  • Do I have to be a Computer Science major?

    Nope, all majors are welcome!
