Event Schedule

Here is our current itenerary. Stay tuned for updates.

Our day will consist of multiple talks and events broken up by breaks for food and games. You and your group will be given ample time to create and at the end of the makeathon, those who created projects will present and prizes will be given out to the winners.

9:00-9:30 AM

Registration

Check-in and Registration will be held in Main Campus New Student Center Commons

9:30-10:00 AM

Welcome Tiffany Nguyen

The Welcoming Ceremony will be held during this time in the Main Campus New Student Center Commons

10:00-11:00 AM

Team Building & Breakfast

This will be a chance for you to participate in team-building exercises with your group. Those of you who want to work in groups but don't currently have a team will get to share your ideas and be matched up with other makers. It will be split between room 234, 236, and 237 of the New Student Center. Breakfast will also be served during this time.

11:00-1:00 PM

Work Time

This will be a time to start or continue working on your projects that will be presented at the end of the day

1:00-2:00 PM

Panel & Lunch Our Speakers

During this time, we will host a panel of judges from different career fields in the Gallery of the New Student Center and pick their brains about topics like artificial intelligence, life hacks, environmental sustainability, community, and health & wellness. There will be an opportunity at the end for Q&A from makers as well. Lunch will also be served, courtesy of The Honors College

2:00-4:00 PM

Work Time

Continue work on your projects

4:00-4:30 PM

Cup-Stacking Tournament

The Cup-Stacking competition will consist of teams of 2 taking turns to stack the highest tower of cups against another team and a racing clock. Winning teams will face-off against eachother in true tournament fashion until only one team remains. Prizes will be rewarded to the finalists so come to rooms 234, 236, and 237 if you want to win!

4:30-6:00 PM

Work Time

Continue work on your projects

6:00-7:00 PM

Dinner

Dinner will be served at this time, courtesy of the Honors College in rooms 234, 236, and 237

7:00-8:00 PM

Work Time

Continue work on your projects

8:00-9:30 PM

Start Presenting Projects

Those who created projects can now begin presenting them to judges in room 253

9:30-10:00 PM

Judging Tiffany Nguyen & Sponsors

Judges will take 30 minutes to decide upon winners in room 253

10:00-10:30 PM

Final Speech and Prizes Awarded Tiffany Nguyen & Sponsors

Return to room 253 for the final speech. Prizes will be awarded at this time

F.A.Q

  • What is ECU Spark?

    ECU Spark is a day of fun and learning. You'll meet awesome people and make cool stuff. There will also be opportunities to win cool prizes and network. Collaborate, Create, Innovate

  • Will there be food?

    YES! Meals will be provided and there will be tons of snacks to get your munch on!

  • Will there be prizes?

    There will be several prizes offered to the people who present their projects! BUT everyone leaves with free swag!

  • Is there a minimum age?

    You must be 18 to attend.

  • What should I bring?

    Bring anything you need to create your awesome project! Some supplies and tools will be provided.

  • Do I have to be a Business or Computer Science major?

    Nope, all majors are welcome!

  • Can I become a sponsor?

    Yes, we'd love to have you! Join the sponsor list by clicking the link in the sponsor section.
